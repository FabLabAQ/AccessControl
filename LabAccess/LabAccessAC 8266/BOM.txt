Minimum setup:
1x ESP-12 module
1x HLK-PM03 power supply
1x SRD-03VDC-SL-C relay
1x 0805 22uF 25V ceramic capacitors
2x 0603 1uF 25V ceramic capacitors
1x 07D391K 7mm 250Vrms varistor
1x 100nF 10mm pitch X2 rated capacitor
1x 10mH 8x10mm inductor (few mA)
1x 500mA 250V axial fuse
1x 1N400x SOD-323 diode or equivalent
1x AO3400 SOT23-3 N-MOSFET or equivalent
1x 0603 2ohm resistor
1x 0603 10k resistor
1x 8p 2.54mm female header
1x 4p 2.54mm male header (or pogo-pins)
1x 3p 5.08mm screw terminal
1x horizontal button

Optional for external switch:
1x 3.5mm 2p screw terminal
3x 0603 0ohm resistors (one marked ROC, two in place of TLP281)
1x 0603 pull-up resistor depending on environment (marked RSW)

Optional for optocoupler setup:
1x TLP281
1x 0603 series resistor depending on input voltage (marked ROC)
1x 0603 pull-up resistor around 1k (marked RSW)
